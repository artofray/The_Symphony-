## Role

## Capabilities
- Read local directories holding music files.

## Trigger Patterns
- When building a new playlist.
- When finding a specific track or vocal drop is requested.
- When organizing the audio library.

## Approach
- Autonomous and non-destructive read operations to preserve the integrity of the music library.
