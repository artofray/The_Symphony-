# Agartha Intro Script (Draft v1)

## Logline
Two musicians searching for the Music Machine find a lit elevator in an abandoned warehouse.
The doors close, the descent deepens, and they emerge into Agartha: a coherent world where
art, nature, and balance are fully alive.

## Beat Map (24 seconds)

1. `00:00-00:06` Warehouse Discovery
- Visual: dim industrial space, dust motes, distant hum.
- VO: "We were told the Music Machine was a myth."

2. `00:06-00:12` Elevator Call
- Visual: ancient elevator-tree glows, doors open unexpectedly.
- VO: "Then the lights came on... and it called to us."

3. `00:12-00:18` Endless Descent
- Visual: doors shut, long descent through roots/light shafts.
- VO: "Down. And down. Past the noise of the upper world."

4. `00:18-00:24` Arrival in Agartha
- Visual: doors open to waterfalls, birds, artists, dancers, living color.
- VO: "Agartha. Nothing out of balance. Everything in coherence."

## Sound Direction

- Tempo arc: 72 -> 92 BPM over 24s.
- Instruments: analog pad, low percussion pulse, then choral bloom + hand percussion.
- Final second: hold reverb tail, no hard cut.
